# Code of conduct

## Our standard

Appwright contributors and maintainers commit to a respectful, harassment-free community. Be
constructive, assume good intent, accept correction gracefully, and focus criticism on ideas and
code rather than people.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, threats, sustained disruption,
publication of another person's private information, sexualized conduct, or retaliation against
someone who raises a concern in good faith.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to **bhargavandhe2310@gmail.com**. Reports will be reviewed
confidentially and fairly. Maintainers may edit or remove contributions, temporarily restrict
participation, or permanently ban participants when necessary to protect the community.

This policy applies in project repositories, discussions, issue trackers, review threads, and
other spaces where someone is representing Appwright.

## Attribution

This policy is informed by the Contributor Covenant and common open-source community standards.
